***** IAS {Indian Administrative Service} *****

👉 To become a IAS step by step.

👉 The Indian Administrative Service is the administrative arm of the All India Service of Government of India, Considered the premier Civil service of india. the IAS is one of the three arms of the All India Service along with the Indian police service and the Indian Forest service. 👍

    👉 Some Requirments for IAS---.

  • IAS Age limit :- 21 to 32 years (Age relaxation as per category) EWS--- 21 TO 32, SC/ST-- 21 TO 37, OBC-- 21 TO 35.
  • Educational Qualification for IAS:- Graduation
  • Nationality:- Indian Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- General {6} OBC {9} SC/ST Unlimited(up tp age limit)

👉 Become a IAS we understand some step.

  • STEP-1 :- Complete High School (10th).
  • STEP-2 :- Complete Intermediate (12th).
  • STEP-3:- After that Complete Graduation(any Stream).
  • STEP-4:- Give UPSC Exam

    👉 UPSC Exam.

    UPSC is India's central agency which conducts exams like Civil Services Exam (CSE) to recruit candidates into top government services like IAS, IPS, IFS etc.
    UPSC conducts three exam
    For IAS
  • 1}--👉 PRELIMINARY EXAM
  • 2}--👉 MAINS EXAMS
  • 3}--👉 INTERVIEW.

    👉 Syllabus of UPSC

    👉 PRELIMINARY EXAM.

  • General Studies paper I (marks 200)

  • Current event of national and internal importance.
  • History of india
  • Indian Polity and governance.
  • Indian and World geography.
  • Economics and social development
  • General issures on environment ecology.
  • General Science

  • General Studies paper II (marks 200)

  • Comprehension
  • Interpersonal skills including Communication skills
  • Logical Reasoning and Analysis ability
  • Decision-makeing and problem-solving
  • General mental ability
  • Basic numeracy(Class-X level)
  • Data Interpretation(Classs-x level)

    👉 MAINS EXAM

  • English(marks 300)

  • Comprehension of given passages.
  • Precis writing.
  • Usage and vocabulary.
  • Short essays.

  • Indian Languages(marks 300)

  • Comprehension of given passages.
  • Precis writing.
  • Usage and vocabulary.
  • Tranlation from English to the Indian Language and vice-versa

  • Essaymarks 250)

  • ANY topic in the given

  • General Studies-I(marks 250)

  • Indian heritage and culture,History and geography of the world and socirty
