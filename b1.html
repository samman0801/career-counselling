👉 BANKING SECTOR step by step.

The modern banking industry is a network of financial institutions licensed by the state to supply banking services. The principal services offered relate to storing, transferring, extending credit against, or managing the risks associated with holding various forms of wealth.

    👉 Some Requirments for Banking Sector.

  • Subject :- 12th With Any Subject
  • Percentages :- 45% to 50% (Percentages)
  • Age:- 18 to 30 years
  • Board:- Any Board
  • UG Course:- Any Stream

👉 Selection Process for Banking Sector.

  • STEP-1 :- Prelims Exam.
  • STEP-2 :- Mains Exam.
  • STEP-2 :- GD(Group Discussion).
  • STEP-2 :- Final Merit List.
