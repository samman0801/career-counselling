***** PCS{Provincial Civil Servic} *****

👉 To become a PCS {Provincial Civil Servic}step by step.

👉 The Provincial Civil Servic is the administrative civil under Group A state service of the executive branch, of The Government of state. pca officers hold carious posts at subdivisional,district,disional and state level from conducting revenue Administration and maintenance of law and order 👍

    👉 Some Requirments for PCS---.

  • PCS Age limit :- 21 to 40 years (Age relaxation as per category) SC/ST,OBC:-- 5 year age limit relaxation
  • Educational Qualification for IAS:- Graduation
  • Nationality:- Indian Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- General {6} OBC {9} SC/ST Unlimited(up tp age limit)

👉 Become a ELectrical Engineer we understand some step.

  • STEP-1 :- Complete High School (10th).
  • STEP-2 :- Complete Intermediate (12th).
  • STEP-3:- After that Complete Graduation(any Stream).
  • STEP-4:- Give UPSC Exam

    👉 UPSC Exam.

    UPSC is India's central agency which conducts exams like Civil Services Exam (CSE) to recruit candidates into top government services .
    UPSC conducts three exam
    For PCS
  • 1}--👉 PRELIMINARY EXAM
  • 2}--👉 MAINS EXAMS
  • 3}--👉 INTERVIEW.

    👉 Syllabus of UPSC

    👉 PRELIMINARY EXAM.

  • General Studies paper I (marks 200)

  • Current event of national and internal importance.
  • History of india
  • Indian Polity and governance.
  • Indian and World geography.
  • Economics and social development
  • General issures on environment ecology.
  • General Science

  • General Studies paper II (marks 200)

  • Comprehension
  • Interpersonal skills including Communication skills
  • Logical Reasoning and Analysis ability
  • Decision-makeing and problem-solving
  • General mental ability
  • Basic numeracy(Class-X level)
  • Data Interpretation(Classs-x level)

    👉 MAINS EXAM

  • General Hindi(marks 150)

  • Shabd gyan evam prayog.
  • Vilom Shabd.
  • Vakyansh k liye ek shabd.
  • Vartani evam vakya shuddhi.
  • Sarkari evam ardhasarkari patra lekhan.
  • Diye huye gadya ka avbodh evam prashnottar.

  • Essays(marks 150)

  • Any topic

  • General Studies-I(marks 200)

  • History of Indian culture
  • Modern History
  • Meaning of liberalization
  • Social Environment
  • Population & Settlements

  • General Studies-II(marks 200)

  • General Studies-III(marks 200)

  • General Studies-IV(marks 200)

  • optional paper 1(marks 200)

  • optional paper 2(marks 200)

  • REGISTRATION STRAT:- 1-21 Febuary.
  • PRELIMINARY Exam:- 28 MAY
  • MAINS Exam:- 15 september
