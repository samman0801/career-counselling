------*****MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)*****------

👉 To become a MBBS (Doctor) step by step.

👉 Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery, is the primary medical degree awarded by medical schools in countries that follow the tradition of the United Kingdom. The historical degree nomenclature states that they are two separate undergraduate degrees. 👍

    👉 Some Requirments for MBBS (Doctor).

  • Education :- Complete 12th (PCB)
  • Min Percentages :- 45% to 50% (Percentages)
  • Age:- Must be 17years old
  • Board:- Any Board
  • Stream:- PCB
  • Course Duration:- 5.5 years

👉 Become a MBBS (Doctor) we understand some step.

  • STEP-1 :- Complete High School (10th).
  • STEP-2 :- Complete Intermediate (12th).
  • STEP-3:- Give some Entrance Exam which is written below.

    👉 Entrance Exam.

  • NEET UG :- Ragistration date 2033 (July to August).
  • NEET PG :- Ragistration date 2033 (August to September).
  • NEET SS:- Ragistration date 2033 (November).
  • AIIMS PG.:- Ragistration date 2033 (March).
  • AIIMS SS:- Ragistration date 2033 (May be - January or November).
  • JIPMER PG:- Ragistration date 2033 (September to October).
