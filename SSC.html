  • SSC GD

  • Age limit :- 18 to 23 years
  • Educational Qualification :- 10th any recoginsed board
  • Nationality:- Indian Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- No limit

  • SSC CHSL

  • Age limit :- 18 to 27 years
  • Educational Qualification :- 12th any recoginsed board
  • Nationality:- Indian,Nepal,bhutan,Tibetan Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- No limit

  • SSC CPO

  • Age limit :- 18 to 25 years (SC/ST,OBC:-- 5 year age limit relaxation)
  • Educational Qualification :- Bachelor's Degree from a recognized university
  • Nationality:- Indian,Nepal,bhutan,Tibetan Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- No limit

  • SSC CGl

  • Age limit :- 18 to 32 years (SC/ST:-- 5 year, OBC:- 3 year age limit relaxation)
  • Educational Qualification :- Bachelor's Degree from a recognized university
  • Nationality:- Indian,Nepal,bhutan,Tibetan Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- No limit

  • SSC CHSL

  • Age limit :- 18 to 25 years
  • Educational Qualification :- 12th any recoginsed board
  • Nationality:- Indian,Nepal,bhutan,Tibetan Citizens only
  • Attemots of Exam:- No limit
