------*****B-PHARMA (Bachelor of Pharmacy)*****------

👉 Done a B-PHARMA step by step.

👉 B Pharma (Bachelor of Pharmacy) is an undergraduate degree course in the area of pharmacy education. This course can be completed in 4 years by the science students after the 12th. B Pharma course consists of the study of a variety of topics related to pharmaceutical science, including drugs & medicines, medicinal chemistry, industrial pharmacy, and other topics. B Pharma degree is appropriate for students who wish to become pharmacists and want to understand how various medicinal medications affect the human body. 👍

    👉 Some Requirments for B-PHARMA.

  • Education :- Complete 12th (PCB)
  • Min Percentages :- 45% to 50% (Percentages)
  • Age:- Must be 17years old
  • Board:- Any Board
  • Stream:- PCB
  • Course Duration:- 4 years

👉 Become a B-PHARMA we understand some step.

  • STEP-1 :- Complete High School (10th).
  • STEP-2 :- Complete Intermediate (12th).
  • STEP-3:- Give some Entrance Exam which is written below.

    👉 Entrance Exam for B-PHARMA.

  • UPSEE :- Ragistration date 2033 (April to May).
  • GPAT :- Ragistration date 2033 (January to Febuary).
  • NEET :- Ragistration date 2033 (November).
  • BITSAT :- Ragistration date 2033 (July).
  • MHT - CET :- Ragistration date 2033 ( April last week or the first week of May).
