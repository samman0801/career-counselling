------*****D-PHARMA (Diploma in Pharmacy)*****------

👉 Done a D-PHARMA step by step.

👉 In India, Diploma in Pharmacy is an entry-level tertiary pharmacy credential. It is obtained following two-year of training . Students can enroll in the course after successfully completing higher secondary education in science stream with physics, chemistry and either biology or maths as subjects 👍

    👉 Some Requirments for D-PHARMA.

  • Education :- Complete 12th (PCB or PCM)
  • Min Percentages :- 50% (Percentages)
  • Age:- Must be 17 years old
  • Board:- Any Board
  • Stream:- PCB
  • Course Duration:- 2 years

👉 Do a D-PHARMA we understand some step.

  • STEP-1 :- Complete High School (10th).
  • STEP-2 :- Complete Intermediate (12th).
  • STEP-3:- Give some Entrance Exam which is written below.

    👉 Entrance Exam for D-PHARMA.

  • UPSEE :- Ragistration date 2033 (April to May).
  • GPAT :- Ragistration date 2033 (January to Febuary).
  • NEET :- Ragistration date 2033 (November).
  • BITSAT :- Ragistration date 2033 (July).
  • MHT - CET :- Ragistration date 2033 ( April last week or the first week of May).
